About

Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee OM KBE FRS FREng FRSA FBCS, also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist
best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. He is a Professorial Fellow of Computer Science at the
University of Oxford and a professor at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
